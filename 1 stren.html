How to Play

Welcome to Sombra The Runner! This is a game of chance where you bet on how long Sombra will run.

  1. Select your bet type: "Greater than" or "Lesser than".
  2. Enter your bet amount.
  3. Enter your predicted odds (how long Sombra will run).
  4. Click "Place Bet" to confirm your bet.
  5. Wait for the game to start and Sombra's run time to be revealed.
  6. If your predicted odds match the actual run time, sombra stop running! Your winnings will be added to your balance.

Rules and Regulations

  • The minimum bet amount is 1 FCFA.
  • The maximum bet amount is your current balance.
  • There is no limits in predicting odds.
  • Each game lasts for 5 seconds.
  • You can only place two bet per game.

How to Win

To win, your predicted odds must meet the following conditions:

  1. Lesser Than Bet: You win if the Current Odds fall within 50% to 100% of your predicted odds.
    Example: If you bet on "Lesser Than" with predicted odds of 10, you win if the Current Odds are between 5 (50% of 10) and 10 (100% of 10).
  2. Greater Than Bet: You win if the Current Odds fall within 100% to 150% of your predicted odds.
    Example: If you bet on "Greater Than" with predicted odds of 10, you win if the Current Odds are between 10 (100% of 10) and 15 (150% of 10).

Remember, these conditions must be met for you to receive a payout. Good luck!
